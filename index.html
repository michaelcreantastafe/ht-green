Geothermal

Energy from the earth. It sounds simple because it is. The earth is warmer than the air, and in some cases hotter than your boiler. HT Green can engineer a solution to let you extract it to power the household — often unseen energy savings year-round.

Geothermal is clean, effective, reliable and environmentally friendly, but has a low-carbon footprint (especially indoors via fan-assisted piping). These systems are extremely efficient, can automatically speed the heat-exchange process, and may qualify for direct applications such as hot water systems and floor/wall heating.

Geothermal is used to release geothermal gases trapped deep within the Earth and turn turbines — this also avoids the use of imported wood fuels, coal, and methane-powered heating, helping mitigate global warming and fuel scarcity.
